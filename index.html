Built for Kenyan Estates

Communest is a digital platform bridging the gap between estate management and residents in Kenya. We provide a transparent, efficient space for listing estates, managing tenants, and resolving issues — all in one place.

Our Mission

Establishment & History

How Communest Came to Be

Communest was founded in 2026 by Kipngeno Shammah Kiplangat, a Nairobi-based software developer with a passion for solving real problems in Kenya's residential property market.

Estate managers were drowning in paper-based systems — handwritten rent receipts, physical complaint books, and phone call chains for announcements. Tenants had no central place to track their lease, payments, or submit issues. Communest was built to close that gap.

A single digital space where an estate can exist online, where outsiders can browse and apply, and where tenants can manage their residency transparently.

📅

2024 — Concept & Research

Identified the gap in Kenya's estate management landscape through field research in Nairobi.

🖥️

2026 — Development & Launch

Full platform development kicks off, bringing together estate management, tenant tooling, and a public listing directory into one cohesive product.

🚀

2026 — Platform Launch

Communest goes live with full tenant management, photo galleries, and public inquiry features.

Legal & Compliance
📜

Title Deeds

Estates are encouraged to register their title deed reference numbers for transparency, verifiable through the Lands Registry.

🏛️

Land Registration Act, 2012

Communest operates in alignment with Kenya's Land Registration Act governing ownership, transfer, and leasing of residential property.

🤝

Tenancy Agreements

Management teams maintain formal lease agreements with all tenants. Communest stores lease dates, rent, and unit details digitally.

🔒

Data Privacy

Tenant data is scoped strictly to their estate. No personal information is shared across estates or made public.

How It Works

Simple for Everyone

Whether you're looking for a home, managing an estate, or already a tenant — Communest has a clear flow for you.

🏙️

1. Browse

Explore all listed estates, filter by location, view photos, and browse available houses — no sign-in required.

📋

2. Apply

Found a house you like? Submit an application directly. Management reviews it and assigns you a unit upon approval.

🏠

3. Manage

Once connected, sign in to your estate dashboard to pay rent, submit requests, view announcements, and manage your lease.

Our Vision

Built for Kenya, Ready for Tomorrow

Communest was built to address a real gap in Kenya's residential market — the lack of a centralised, digital-first platform where estate managers and tenants can operate transparently and efficiently.

The platform covers the full lifecycle of tenancy: public listings, house browsing and applications, tenant onboarding, rent tracking, maintenance requests, announcements, and photo galleries — all in one place.

The goal from day one has been a product that genuinely works for Kenyan estates of every size — from compact residential blocks to large gated communities.

🌍

Kenya-First Design

Built with Kenyan estates, landlords, and tenants in mind — from local payment flows to alignment with the Land Registration Act.
